Summary

The Shawshank Redemption follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, as he builds a life inside Shawshank State Penitentiary. Over the years Andy forms a deep friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding, quietly brings hope to the prison through small acts of defiance and skill, and ultimately plans a patient, daring escape that changes both men's lives.

Actors

  • Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne — A calm, intelligent banker who endures prison life with quiet resilience.
  • Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding — The film's narrator and Andy's close friend; wise and compassionate.
  • Bob Gunton as Warden Norton — The pious but corrupt warden who maintains strict control of Shawshank.
  • William Sadler as Heywood — One of the inmates and a friend within the prison community.

Favorite Quote

"Get busy living, or get busy dying." — Andy Dufresne

This line captures the film's core message about hope, choice, and the will to survive against all odds.
