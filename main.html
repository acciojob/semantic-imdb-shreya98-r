Movie Summary

A young archaeologist named Maya discovers a mysterious map hidden inside an ancient book. The map leads her to a forgotten city buried deep in the Amazon rainforest. With the help of her childhood friend, she sets off on a dangerous journey filled with puzzles, traps, and rival treasure hunters. As Maya unlocks the secrets of the city, she realizes it’s not just about treasure—it’s about protecting a powerful artifact that could change the fate of humanity. In the end, she must decide between personal glory and saving the world from falling into the wrong hands.
